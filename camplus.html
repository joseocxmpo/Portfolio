Camplus

Camplus is a mobile application designed to enhance the university experience for students. It serves as a comprehensive platform that provides access to relevant information, facilitates social interaction among students, and offers additional resources and services to support academic life. Camplus aims to streamline communication, improve engagement, and create a connected community within the university environment.

Research

Problem

University students often face challenges integrating into a new academic system, struggling to stay informed, find their place, and access relevant information efficiently. This can result in feelings of isolation, missed opportunities, and a suboptimal university experience.

Findings

Based on insights gathered from 5 user interviews and 5 survey responses, the following key findings emerged:

  1. Information overload: Students struggle to stay updated on important events and notices due to the overwhelming amount of information from various sources.
  2. Fragmented communication: Students face challenges with fragmented communication channels, leading to missed opportunities and limited community engagement.
  3. Social integration: Newcomers and international students desire better social integration, seeking platforms that foster connections and collaboration with peers.

Goal

The goal of Camplus is to enhance the university experience for students by providing a comprehensive platform that facilitates communication, promotes social integration, and offers easy access to relevant information, resources, and opportunities. Camplus aims to simplify and streamline the university journey, fostering a sense of belonging and empowering students to make the most of their academic experience.
